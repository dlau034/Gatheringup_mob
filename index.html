The many dimensions of Bethesda Terrace

Local Visitors

Enter Central Park at West 72nd street, wander through Strawberry Fields and climb over Cherry Hill, and you’ll be standing in Bethesda Terrace, one of the most iconic spots in all of Central Park. Known for its fountain, which features the Angel of the Waters statue, Bethesda Terrace is a popular place for park goers of all types to gather.
